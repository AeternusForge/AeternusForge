Your privacy matters. This policy explains what information AeternusForge (aeternusforge.com) collects, how it's used, and your choices. Last updated: July 19, 2026.

**The short version:** this is a static website. I don't ask for your name, I don't run a newsletter, and I don't sell data. The only information involved is the standard, anonymous data any website and its service providers handle automatically.

## Information collected automatically

Like most sites, this one is served through Cloudflare, which may log standard technical details such as your IP address, browser type, device, and pages visited — used only to keep the site secure and running well and to understand general traffic. It isn't tied to your identity.

## Cookies

This site doesn't set tracking cookies to identify you. However:

- Cloudflare may set essential cookies needed for security and performance.
- When you click an affiliate link (such as an Amazon link), that retailer may set cookies to credit the referral. Those are governed by the retailer's own privacy policy.

You can block or delete cookies in your browser settings at any time.

## Third-party services

- **Hosting & security:** Cloudflare.
- **Affiliate links:** Amazon and other affiliate partners — see the Affiliate Disclosure for details.

## Children's privacy

This site isn't directed at children under 13 and doesn't knowingly collect information from them.
